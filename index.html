Better Than Wolves is an overhaul of Minecraft developed by Flowerchild that enhances the game with advanced technological progression and hardcore survival elements. Following Flowerchild's retirement in 2021, the project has continued through various mods and addons as well as the Better Than Wolves: Community Edition.
